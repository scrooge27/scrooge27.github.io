INKTOBER

Inktober is an art challenge created by comic artist Jake Parker in 2009. It consists in making 31 inked drawings through the month of October, each of them being also related to a specific prompt. It is an extraordinary opportunity to join a worldwide community of artists.

I grew up reading my father's comics so I couldn't help becoming a Marvel fan and here you can see a selection of my best artworks regarding superheroes. As you will notice, the characters I like the most are Daken, Wolverine's son, and, obviously, Spider-Man.

VARIOUS

Born in 2002, since 2014 I've been keeping practising in drawing in order to become a comic book artist. I've taken part into the Inktober challenge for 4 years now and my improvements can be seen in the ARTBOOKS section.

I have an addiction for MARVEL characters and for Disney's Uncle Scrooge. I have also collaborated with my school's team in the Mad for Science competition, providing digital paintings for the chemistry lab.

I am currently working as illustrator on an unannounced secret project based on greek mythology.
